What Is Virtuosi??

The dictionary says that “Virtuosi” means a group of skilled musicians or artists. Well... we might not be professionals but we play what we play from the bottom of our hearts and melody runs through our veins. Virtuosi is a place where all the musical souls that arrive at IIITA gather and express their infinitesimal love for music!...
